Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Parvaly LLC ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit parvaly.com or use our digital marketing consulting services.

1) About us

Parvaly LLC is a Wyoming-registered limited liability company providing digital marketing consulting services to businesses in the United States. All services are delivered remotely.

2) Information we collect

We may collect information you provide to us, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (when provided)
  • Business details you share (e.g., company name, website URL, Google Business Profile link)
  • Your message or service request details

We may also collect basic technical information automatically, such as:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (e.g., country or city)

3) How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Provide and deliver our digital marketing consulting services
  • Process payments through our payment processor (Stripe)
  • Communicate with you about your account or services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Protect our website from misuse

4) Payment processing

We use Stripe as our payment processor for credit/debit card and ACH payments. When you make a payment, your payment information is collected and processed directly by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card number or bank account details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

5) Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for website functionality and analytics. For details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, see our Cookie Policy.

6) How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Website hosting providers
  • Email service providers
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics)

We may also share information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

7) Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations.

8) Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at support@parvaly.com. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

9) Data location

Parvaly LLC is a US company serving US clients. Our team works remotely, and your data may be processed in the United States and in countries where our service providers operate.

10) Children's privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
