## DESIGN & UX RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **Color scheme:** Dark navy/charcoal primary, green or blue accents for CTAs (high contrast). Matches professional/trust feel.
2. **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, DM Sans, or similar). Headlines bold, body regular.
3. **Form visibility:** The audit form should appear 3 times — hero, mid-page, and final block.
4. **Mobile-first:** 60%+ traffic will be mobile. All blocks should stack cleanly.
5. **Page speed:** Keep it lightweight. No heavy animations. Fast load = better conversions.
6. **Trust signals throughout:** Google Partner badge (if applicable), "No Risk" badges, "Cancel Anytime" repeated.
7. **Social proof placement:** Testimonials/stats should appear between blocks 4 and 7 to reinforce value before pricing.
