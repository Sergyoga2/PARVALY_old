Refund Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how Parvaly LLC handles refunds for paid services.

1) When refunds may be available

Refund requests are reviewed case-by-case and depend on the specific service and scope agreed in writing.

If you paid for a service and we have not started work, you may request a refund.

2) Non-refundable situations

Refunds are generally not available if:

  • A one-time audit has been delivered
  • Work has already started (e.g., analysis completed, recommendations prepared, implementation begun)
  • The delay or inability to deliver is caused by missing access, missing information, or lack of response from the client

3) Monthly services (if applicable)

If you have an ongoing monthly service, you can cancel future periods according to the agreement. Fees for the current billing period are generally non-refundable once the period has started.

4) How to request a refund

Send your request to:
support@parvaly.com

Include:

  • Your name
  • The service you purchased
  • Date of payment
  • Reason for the request

We will respond within a reasonable time.

5) Chargebacks

If you have an issue, please contact us first. We will do our best to resolve it quickly.
