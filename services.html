Important Information

No Guarantees of Rankings

We do not guarantee specific search rankings, traffic volumes, or revenue outcomes. Search engine algorithms, market conditions, and other factors outside our control affect results. We commit to professional, consistent work and transparent reporting.

Ad Spend Is Separate

When we manage advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads), clients pay the ad platforms directly from their own accounts. We do not handle client funds. Our fees cover consulting and campaign management services only.

We accept payments via Stripe (credit and debit cards) and ACH bank transfers. All services are billed monthly with no long-term contracts.

Remote Service Delivery

All services are delivered remotely. We serve clients across the entire United States via email and messaging platforms.
